About TIGO

Company Overview

Tigo is a leading telecommunications provider, committed to delivering reliable and innovative mobile and internet services to millions of customers across the region. Founded in 2005, Tigo has grown to become a trusted name in connectivity, offering a wide range of services that empower individuals and businesses alike.

Our Mission

To connect people and businesses with cutting-edge technology, providing them with the tools they need to thrive in an increasingly connected world. Tigo is dedicated to innovation, customer satisfaction, and building a more connected future.

Our Values

At Tigo, we are driven by core values that guide everything we do: integrity, innovation, customer focus, and community involvement. We believe in creating opportunities for growth and development for our customers, employees, and the communities we serve.
